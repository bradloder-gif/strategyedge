# StrategyEdge — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Corporate Precision

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets Corporate Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
- Strict typographic hierarchy with generous whitespace
- Information presented in structured grids with clear visual lanes
- Restraint in decoration — every element earns its place
- Authority conveyed through precision, not ornamentation

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#1A2B5E) as the dominant authority colour, with accent blue (#3A6BC4) used sparingly for emphasis. White and off-white backgrounds create breathing room. The palette communicates trustworthiness and institutional credibility.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric column layouts — a wide content column paired with a narrow accent column. Navigation anchored left. Sections divided by full-width navy bands rather than decorative elements.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule lines in navy as section dividers
- Bold uppercase section labels in small caps with wide letter-spacing
- Accent square motif (echoing the PPT corner accent) used as decorative punctuation

**Interaction Philosophy:** Subtle, purposeful. Hover states reveal underlines or colour shifts. No gratuitous animation — motion is reserved for meaningful transitions.

**Animation:** Fade-in on scroll for content blocks. Underline draw animation on nav hover. No bouncing or playful effects.

**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (bold, uppercase) for headings; Source Serif 4 for body text; Barlow for UI labels.
</text>

## Approach 2: Executive Edge — CHOSEN

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Executive Consulting — editorial precision with structural boldness

**Core Principles:**
- Bold typographic contrasts create visual hierarchy without relying on decoration
- Diagonal and angular structural elements echo the "edge" in the brand name
- Deep navy dominates, with strategic use of accent blue for calls-to-action and highlights
- Content density balanced with deliberate whitespace — authoritative but not overwhelming

**Color Philosophy:** Navy (#1A2B5E) as the foundation of authority and trust. Accent blue (#3A6BC4) as the active, forward-moving colour. A subtle warm off-white (#F8F7F4) for section backgrounds adds tactile warmth against the clinical navy. Gold/amber accent (#C9A84C) as a tertiary highlight for premium differentiation.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with diagonal clip-path transition into content. Alternating left/right content-image layouts for the profile section. A horizontal scrolling client logo strip. Sticky top navigation that transitions from transparent to solid navy on scroll.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section dividers using CSS clip-path (echoing the "edge" concept)
- Vertical text labels on section sides (rotated 90°) in small caps
- A thin left-border accent line on blockquotes and key statements

**Interaction Philosophy:** Confident and smooth. Hover effects on cards use a subtle upward lift with shadow deepening. CTA buttons have a sliding fill animation. Navigation items use an underline that grows from left.

**Animation:** Entrance animations on scroll using Framer Motion (fade + slide up). Staggered reveal for client logos. Smooth section transitions.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (bold italic) for hero headlines; Barlow (medium/semibold) for section headings; Inter (regular/light) for body text. This creates an editorial feel that signals premium consulting.
</text>

## Approach 3: Structural Blueprint

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Technical Blueprint meets Financial Services Precision

**Core Principles:**
- Grid-exposed layouts that make the structural logic visible
- Monochromatic navy palette with single accent for maximum professionalism
- Data-forward presentation — the experience feels like a well-designed annual report
- Typography does the heavy lifting; imagery is secondary

**Color Philosophy:** Near-monochromatic navy spectrum — from #0D1B3E (darkest) to #E8EDF7 (lightest tint). A single electric blue (#2563EB) for interactive elements. No warm tones — this is a cold, precise, analytical brand.

**Layout Paradigm:** Exposed grid lines as decorative elements. Section numbers in large, faded numerals behind content. A sidebar navigation on desktop that tracks scroll position.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large faded section numerals (01, 02, 03) as background watermarks
- Dotted grid pattern as subtle texture on dark sections
- Monospace font for data points and statistics

**Interaction Philosophy:** Analytical and precise. Hover states reveal data or expand content. Progress indicators on scroll.

**Animation:** Counting animations for statistics. Grid line draw animations on section entry. Minimal but precise.

**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (bold) for headings; IBM Plex Sans for body; IBM Plex Mono for data points and statistics.
</text>

## Selected Approach: **Executive Edge (Approach 2)**

The Executive Edge approach best reflects the StrategyEdge brand — authoritative, forward-moving, and premium. The diagonal "edge" motif directly ties to the brand name, while the editorial typography (Playfair Display + Barlow) signals high-calibre consulting without feeling stiff or bureaucratic.
